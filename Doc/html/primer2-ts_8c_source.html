155 /* Simple model of primer2 touch screen:
156  Vdd-[R1]-[Ry]-[Rp]-[Rx]-Vss
157  U D l R
158  where R1=1k external register, Rx(resp. Ry)=resisitive component on
159  X(resp. Y) film and Rp=resisitive component of contact point.
160  Convert [L, R, U, D] to [Rx, Ry, Rp]. */
161 void
